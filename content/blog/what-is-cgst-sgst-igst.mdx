# What is CGST, SGST and IGST?

GST in India has three components: **CGST** (Central GST), **SGST** (State GST), and **IGST** (Integrated GST).

## CGST

Central Goods and Services Tax collected by the **Central Government** on intrastate supply. Rate = Half the total GST rate.

## SGST

State Goods and Services Tax collected by the **State Government** on intrastate supply. Rate = Half the total GST rate.

## IGST

Integrated Goods and Services Tax collected by the **Central Government** on interstate supply. Rate = Full GST rate.

## When to Apply?

- **Same state** → CGST + SGST (each at half rate)
- **Different states** → IGST (at full rate)

## Example at 18% GST

**Intrastate** (e.g., Mumbai to Pune):
- CGST: 9% = ₹9,000 on ₹1,00,000
- SGST: 9% = ₹9,000
- Total Tax: ₹18,000

**Interstate** (e.g., Mumbai to Delhi):
- IGST: 18% = ₹18,000 on ₹1,00,000
- Total Tax: ₹18,000

## GST Rate Slabs

| Rate | Examples |
|---|---|
| 0% | Fresh produce, books |
| 5% | Basic necessities |
| 12% | Processed foods |
| 18% | IT services, most goods |
