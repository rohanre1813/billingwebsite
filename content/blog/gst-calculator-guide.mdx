# GST Calculator Guide — How to Calculate GST in India

Calculating GST manually can be confusing, especially with CGST, SGST, and IGST split. This guide shows you exactly how to calculate GST and use our free online GST calculator.

## The Basic GST Formula

### Adding GST to a price:
- **GST Amount** = (Base Amount × GST Rate) ÷ 100
- **Total Amount** = Base Amount + GST Amount

### Removing GST from an inclusive price:
- **Base Amount** = (Total Amount × 100) ÷ (100 + GST Rate)
- **GST Amount** = Total Amount − Base Amount

## Examples

**Add 18% GST to ₹10,000:**
- GST = (10,000 × 18) ÷ 100 = **₹1,800**
- Total = 10,000 + 1,800 = **₹11,800**
- CGST = ₹900, SGST = ₹900 (intrastate)
- OR IGST = ₹1,800 (interstate)

**Remove 18% GST from ₹11,800:**
- Base = (11,800 × 100) ÷ 118 = **₹10,000**
- GST = 11,800 − 10,000 = **₹1,800**
