## Step-by-Step: Creating Your GST Invoice

To follow along, open the **[JusBill GST Invoice Generator](/gst-invoice-generator)** in a new tab.

### Step 1: Add Your Business Details (The Supplier)
Start by filling out your own details. This forms the header of the invoice.
*   **Your Name / Company Name:** Enter your legal business name.
*   **Your Address:** Provide your complete registered billing address.
*   **Your GSTIN:** If you are a GST-registered business, enter your 15-digit GSTIN here. If you are unregistered, you can leave this blank.
*   **State:** *Crucial Step!* Ensure you select the correct state from the dropdown. This is required for the tool to calculate intrastate vs. interstate tax.

### Step 2: Add Customer Details (The Recipient)
Next, enter the details of the person or business you are billing.
*   **Customer Name:** The legal name of the buyer.
*   **Customer Address:** Where the goods/services are being delivered or billed.
*   **Customer GSTIN:** If you are doing B2B (Business to Business) sales, enter their GSTIN so they can claim Input Tax Credit (ITC).
*   **State:** Again, select the customer's state. If this state is different from *your* state, the tool will automatically apply IGST.

### Step 3: Set Invoice Meta-Data
*   **Invoice Number:** Enter a unique, sequential invoice number (e.g., INV-2024-001).
*   **Invoice Date:** The date of issue (defaults to today).

### Step 4: Add Your Items or Services
This is the core of the bill. You can add as many items as you need by clicking the "Add Item" button.
*   **Item Description:** Clearly describe the good or service (e.g., "Website Design Services" or "Dell XPS 15 Laptop").
*   **Quantity:** How many units?
*   **Price:** The base price *before* tax.
*   **GST %:** Select the applicable GST slab for this specific item (0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, or 28%).

*Watch the magic happen:* As you add items, the tool instantly calculates the subtotal, the exact tax breakdown (CGST/SGST or IGST), and the grand total. It even converts the final amount into words automatically!

### Step 5: Add Payment Details and Notes
Make it easy for your client to pay you.
*   **Bank Details / UPI:** Enter your Bank Account Number, IFSC code, or your UPI ID in the notes section.
*   **Terms & Conditions:** Add standard terms like "Payment due within 15 days" or "Goods once sold will not be taken back."

### Step 6: Preview and Download
Take a quick look at the live preview generated on the screen. It updates in real-time as you type. If everything looks correct, click the **"Download PDF"** button.

Your browser will instantly download a clean, professional, print-ready PDF invoice that is fully compliant with Indian GST laws. You can now email this directly to your client or print a physical copy.
