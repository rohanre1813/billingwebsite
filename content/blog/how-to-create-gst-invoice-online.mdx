## What is a GST Invoice?

A GST invoice is a commercial document issued by a registered supplier of goods or services. It contains all the details of the transaction — including the seller's and buyer's GSTIN, the list of goods/services, quantity, price, and the applicable GST (CGST, SGST, or IGST).

Under the GST Act, every registered business **must issue a GST invoice** for every taxable supply.

## Who Needs to Issue a GST Invoice?

- Businesses registered under GST
- Freelancers providing services above ₹20 lakh turnover
- Shopkeepers and retailers selling taxable goods
- Startups and small businesses making B2B transactions

## Required Fields in a GST Invoice

A valid GST invoice in India must contain the following information:

1. **Supplier details** — Name, address, and GSTIN
2. **Buyer details** — Name, address, and GSTIN (for B2B transactions)
3. **Invoice number** — Consecutive and unique
4. **Invoice date** — Date of issue
5. **Description of goods/services** — With HSN/SAC code
6. **Quantity and unit**
7. **Taxable value**
8. **GST rate and amount** — Separately showing CGST, SGST (intrastate) or IGST (interstate)
9. **Total invoice value**
10. **Signature of the supplier**

## GST Invoice Format Tips

- Use a **consecutive invoice numbering** system (e.g., INV-2024-001, INV-2024-002)
- Always mention the **HSN code** for goods and **SAC code** for services
- For intrastate supply, split the tax as **CGST + SGST** (each at half the GST rate)
- For interstate supply, charge **IGST** at the full GST rate
- Mention **"Tax Invoice"** clearly at the top of the document

## Common GST Invoice Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Wrong supply type** — Always check if the transaction is intrastate or interstate
2. **Missing GSTIN** — Required for B2B invoices
3. **Incorrect HSN codes** — Use the correct 4 or 8-digit HSN code
4. **Not mentioning CGST and SGST separately** — They must be shown as separate tax lines
5. **Wrong invoice date** — Invoice must be issued within 30 days of supply for goods (45 days for services)
